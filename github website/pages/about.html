Happy Wang

Hi there! I'm Happy Wang, a student at USC majoring in Business of Cinematic Arts and minoring in User Experience and Themed Entertainment. My passion lies at the intersection of storytelling, marketing, and creating immersive experiences that connect audiences around the globe.

At high school, my love for film drove me to independently produce a 15-minute documentary selected by multiple film festivals. Additionally, I've spent over six years as a content creator—filming vlogs, writing blogs and reviews, and exploring photography and design.

I'm currently building a career in entertainment marketing, where I thrive at the crossroads of creativity and strategy—whether crafting campaigns or designing engaging experiences, I'm always looking for ways to inspire and resonate with others.

Outside of these pursuits, you'll often find me enjoying stand-up comedy (I'm a huge fan!), exploring museums and theme parks, or experimenting with Chinese recipes in my kitchen.
